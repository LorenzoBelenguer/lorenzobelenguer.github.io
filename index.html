Biography

Lorenzo Belenguer holds an MA in Philosophy & Artificial Intelligence (ethics focus) from Northeastern University – London, and a BA in Economics from the University of Valencia. Research interests include ethical frameworks as frameworks of action, bias leading to discriminatory outcomes, human-AI interaction, and machine learning for public benefit—especially for historically marginalised communities.

Lorenzo identifies as nonbinary and is also a visual artist; exhibitions include the British Museum and Tate Modern. Based in London for two decades, they advocate for the power of the arts to build empathy and for AI to be governed transparently, fairly, and inclusively.
